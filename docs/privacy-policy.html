Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 30, 2025
Effective date: November 30, 2025
Version: 1.0

Introduction

Chipa SSID Finder ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our services.

Information We Don't Collect

Chipa SSID Finder does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically, we do NOT:

  • Collect login credentials or passwords
  • Store authentication tokens or SSID data
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Collect device information
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use advertising or tracking services
  • Require user accounts or registration

How the App Works

  1. WebView Login: When you select a trading platform, the app opens a secure WebView where you log in directly to the trading platform's website
  2. Cookie Extraction: After successful login, the app extracts only the session cookie (SSID token) from the WebView
  3. Local Display: The SSID is displayed only on your device
  4. Clipboard Copy: When you copy the SSID, it's stored in your device's clipboard temporarily
  5. No Storage: Once you close the app or navigate away, the SSID is cleared and not stored anywhere

Data Storage

  • No Cloud Storage: Nothing is uploaded to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • No Local Database: The app doesn't save any data to your device storage
  • Temporary Memory Only: SSID tokens exist only in the app's temporary memory while displayed
  • WebView Cookies: Trading platform cookies are managed by the Android WebView component and follow standard browser behavior

Third-Party Services

Trading Platforms

When you log in to trading platforms (OlympTrade, PocketOptions, etc.), you are subject to their respective privacy policies:

  • Your interaction with these platforms is direct
  • We do not intercept, modify, or store your credentials
  • Session data is handled according to each platform's policies

Android WebView

The app uses Android's WebView component, which is provided and maintained by Google. WebView follows Android's standard privacy and security practices.

Permissions

The app requests only necessary permissions:

Internet Permission

  • Why: Required to load trading platform websites in the WebView
  • Usage: Only used to display web pages you explicitly navigate to

Network State Permission

  • Why: To check if device has internet connectivity
  • Usage: To show appropriate messages if offline

Security

  • Secure WebView: All connections use HTTPS where supported by the trading platform
  • No Data Transmission: Your data never leaves your device through our app
  • Session Isolation: Each WebView session is independent
  • No Tracking: We don't implement any analytics or tracking

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is nothing to:

  • Access
  • Delete
  • Modify
  • Export
  • Request

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Publishing changes on our GitHub repository

Data Deletion

Since we don't store any data, there is no data to delete. If you want to remove all traces:

  1. Uninstall the app
  2. Clear your device's clipboard (if you copied SSID recently)
  3. Clear browser cookies for the trading platforms you accessed

Legal Compliance

  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA)
  • Other applicable privacy laws

Users are responsible for:
